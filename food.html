Delicious Spanish Cuisine

Spanish food is known for its bold flavors, fresh ingredients, and regional diversity.

Paella

This famous rice dish from Valencia comes in many varieties including seafood, mixed, and vegetarian.

Tapas

Small plates meant for sharing, including patatas bravas, jamón ibérico, and tortilla española.

Gazpacho

A refreshing cold tomato soup perfect for hot summer days.

Churros con Chocolate

Fried dough pastries served with thick hot chocolate for dipping.

Regional Specialties

Each region has its own specialties like fabada asturiana (bean stew) or pulpo a la gallega (Galician octopus).

Wines & Drinks

Enjoy Rioja wines, sherry from Jerez, or the refreshing sangria.
