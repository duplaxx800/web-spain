Must-Visit Destinations in Spain

Spain offers diverse attractions from historic cities to beautiful beaches and stunning natural landscapes.

Barcelona

Visit Gaudi's masterpiece Sagrada Familia, Park Güell, and the vibrant Las Ramblas.

Madrid

Explore the Prado Museum, Royal Palace, and Retiro Park in Spain's capital.

Seville

See the Alcázar palace, Seville Cathedral, and experience authentic flamenco.

Granada

Marvel at the Alhambra palace and enjoy the unique atmosphere of the Albayzín.

Coastal Beauties

Relax on Costa del Sol beaches or hike the dramatic cliffs of Costa Brava.

Canary Islands

Enjoy year-round sunshine, volcanic landscapes, and unique ecosystems.
